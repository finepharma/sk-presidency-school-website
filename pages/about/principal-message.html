Principal's Message

A Word from Our Leadership

👔

Shesh Mani Mishra

Principal

Experience: 20+ Years in Education

Qualification: M.Ed, B.Ed

"It is my proud privilege to welcome you into the prospectus of this learning community, SK PRESIDENCY PUBLIC SCHOOL, Sultanpur, dedicated to develop skilled and outstanding students who can pursue their interests and strengths to the best of their ability so that our enriched students merge into the society building a strong generation for a better world through dynamic education."

Our curriculum, course offering, co-curricular activities aim towards growth of our students. We are committed to work collaboratively with parents to nurture an individual who possesses looks for nation, society, community and family values.

We also expect that we as a family will encourage students to persevere in case of challenges and adversity never give up spirit. Our school is such a place where rumbling bell of almighty, voice of parents and community is heard.

We welcome your ideas, thoughts and concerns. All of this is possible by the strong partnership between school, staff and the parents. 'Quality education for all' is our guiding principle and we constantly look up on Almighty for strength and wisdom.

We hope that your confidence in us will assist in this endeavor. Thank you!

Shesh Mani Mishra

Principal, SK Presidency Public School
