About Our Curriculum

SK Presidency Public School follows the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, which is recognized nationally and internationally. Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education combining theoretical knowledge with practical skills development.

Pre-Primary (Nursery - KG)

  • Language Development
  • Mathematical Concepts
  • Environmental Studies
  • Art and Craft
  • Music and Dance
  • Physical Education
  • Play-based Learning

Primary (Class I - V)

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Science
  • Computer Studies
  • Art Education
  • Physical Education

Middle School (Class VI - VIII)

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Science
  • Computer Studies
  • Art & Music
  • Physical Education

Senior Secondary (Class IX - XII)

Science Stream:

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Mathematics
  • English, Hindi

Commerce Stream:

  • Accounts, Economics
  • Business Studies
  • English, Hindi
